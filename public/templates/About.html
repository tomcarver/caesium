Welcome to Caesium time-tracking

Caesium is a time-tracking tool that just gets out of your way. To start recording time, just hit "start recording" (up in the top-right of the screen). To stop, hit "stop". When you change task, just change the description of your current task - or if you're in a rush just hit 'new task' and update the description later. Caesium records when you start/stop to make totting up how you spend your time trivial.

There's no installation, and no account set-up - you're all set. Caesium stores all your data on your own device. Caesium is free and always will be - if you want, grab the code and host it locally. However we plan to offer paid accounts in future so your team can collaborate and sync data between devices. If you're interested Get in touch!
